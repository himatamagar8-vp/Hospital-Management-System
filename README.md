 **20 features with very small explanation**:

1. **Patient Registration** – Add new patient details.
2. **Patient Search** – Find patient by name or ID.
3. **Doctor Registration** – Add doctor information.
4. **Doctor Search** – Search doctor by name or department.
5. **Appointment Booking** – Set appointment between doctor and patient.
6. **Appointment List** – Show all scheduled appointments.
7. **Cancel Appointment** – Delete an appointment.
8. **Add Medical Record** – Save diagnosis and treatment details.
9. **View Medical Records** – Show patient’s medical history.
10. **Room Management** – Assign or free rooms for patients.
11. **Billing System** – Calculate total treatment charges.
12. **Payment Collection** – Mark bill as paid or unpaid.
13. **Staff Registration** – Add hospital staff details.
14. **Staff Search** – Find staff by role or ID.
15. **Lab Test Entry** – Record lab test results.
16. **Medicine Inventory** – Manage medicine stock.
17. **Reports Module** – Show statistics like total patients.
18. **Login System** – Admin/staff authentication.
19. **Activity Log** – Track actions done in system.
20. **Data Export** – Export data to text file.
